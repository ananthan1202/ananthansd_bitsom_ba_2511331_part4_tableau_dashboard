# Executive Dashboard Story

Executive Summary

This executive dashboard provides leadership with a consolidated view of sales performance, profitability, customer segments, shipping efficiency, discount impact, and product returns. It enables informed business decisions using interactive visualizations.

What is Performing Well

Overall sales remain healthy, with the North region contributing significantly to revenue. Technology products demonstrate strong profitability, and Corporate customers generate high sales volumes.

Underperforming Areas

Some categories show lower profitability despite good sales. Higher discounts reduce profit margins, while certain shipping modes have longer delivery times.

Business Risks

High return rates and increasing discounts present financial risks. Delivery delays may negatively impact customer satisfaction and future sales.

Business Opportunities

Opportunities include expanding successful regional strategies, promoting high-profit products, improving logistics, and optimizing pricing strategies.

Recommended Business Actions

- Focus marketing on profitable categories.
- Reduce excessive discounts.
- Improve shipping efficiency.
- Investigate products with high return rates.
- Expand best-performing regional practices.

Limitations

The dashboard uses historical transactional data and does not include inventory levels, customer feedback, or external market conditions.

Suggested Next Analysis

Future work should include sales forecasting, customer lifetime value analysis, demand prediction, inventory optimization, and predictive analytics.

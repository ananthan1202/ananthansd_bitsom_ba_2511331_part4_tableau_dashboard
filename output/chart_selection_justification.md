# Chart Selection Justification

Sales Trend (Line Chart)

Business Question: How have sales changed over time?

Reason: A line chart clearly displays trends and seasonal variations.

Fields Used: Order Date (Columns), Sales (Rows)

Design Principle: Simple time-series visualization with clear labels.

Mistake Avoided: Avoided bar charts for continuous time analysis.

---

Regional Performance (Bar Chart)

Business Question: Which region performs best?

Reason: Bar charts allow easy comparison between regions.

Fields Used: Region, Sales, Profit

Design Principle: Sorted bars improve readability.

Mistake Avoided: Avoided pie charts for multiple category comparison.

---

Category Profitability (Horizontal Bar Chart)

Business Question: Which categories generate the highest profit?

Reason: Horizontal bars effectively compare multiple categories.

Fields Used: Sub Category, Profit

Design Principle: Color highlights profitability.

Mistake Avoided: Avoided overcrowded charts.

---

Customer Segment (Bar Chart)

Business Question: Which customer segment contributes the most sales?

Reason: Bar charts simplify comparison across customer groups.

Fields Used: Customer Segment, Sales

Design Principle: Consistent colors and labels.

Mistake Avoided: Avoided unnecessary visual complexity.

---

Discount vs Profit (Scatter Plot)

Business Question: How do discounts affect profit?

Reason: Scatter plots effectively reveal relationships between two numerical variables.

Fields Used: Discount, Profit

Design Principle: Individual points reveal trends.

Mistake Avoided: Avoided misleading aggregated charts.

---

Shipping Performance (Bar Chart)

Business Question: Which shipping mode performs best?

Reason: Bar charts clearly compare average delivery times.

Fields Used: Ship Mode, Delivery Days

Design Principle: Clear axis labels and consistent formatting.

Mistake Avoided: Avoided confusing multiple-axis charts.

---

Return Analysis (Bar Chart)

Business Question: Which categories experience the highest returns?

Reason: Bar charts make return comparisons straightforward.

Fields Used: Category, Return Flag

Design Principle: Simple comparison with readable labels.

Mistake Avoided: Avoided excessive colors and clutter.
